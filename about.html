ABOUT US

City Hospital was established in 2019, with the mission of providing specialized tertiary medical care services at affordable prices to under served communities across the country. This private healthcare provider stands apart from others in it’s vision of making healthcare for all a reality for ordinary people in rural and remote regions. Every single individual working in a ‘City Hospital’ Hospital- from doctors to nurses and the management- is united in the endeavor to deliver top quality healthcare to all. City Hospital is a leader in establishing specialized hospitals in places that lack access to healthcare, specifically super specialty tertiary care. Each of it’s initiative is based on three tenets of healthcare- Affordability, Accessibility & Quality.

OUR MISSION


Our mission is to provide the best quality medical service with utmost responsibility and compassion. Our management ensures that every patient gets the best possible treatment whether preventive or diagnostic.

OUR VISION

City Hospitals aspires to be the preferred healthcare partner for the community. We will initiate all our endeavors based on the 3 tenets of healthcare – Affordability, Accessibility and Quality.
